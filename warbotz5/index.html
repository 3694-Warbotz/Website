NAHS Warbotz

We are the North Atlanta Warbotz, a high school robotics team that combines STEM and teamwork to foster academic growth and skill.


Every year, we come together to brainstorm, plan, and build an efficient and competitive robot for FIRST, an international youth organization that operates the First Robotics Competition, First Lego League, First Lego League Jr., and First Tech Challenge competitions.
